The Imperial recommendation algorithm

-creation of subcategories in database

composed of to components: 
    first the item recommendation: use a classifier to rank item likability. 
    it is inputed the specs of an item and the specs of a user (see getData.py for more) and output a sigmoid score for the likability 

    the second is the price regression algorithm. Using price data on certain subcategories, it gets a likalability score on how 
    the product is fairly priced (0 = to much ask, 1 = cheap)
